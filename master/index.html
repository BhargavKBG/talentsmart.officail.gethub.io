AUTOMOBILE

The Indian automobile industry is the fourth largest automobile sector in the world. The automobile industry has a steep growth with the rise in the middle-class income. The two-wheeler segment is becoming more popular due to the young population and middle-class disposable income. India is expected to become a leader in the automobile industry by 2030. This boom in the automobile industry is a sign of opportunities for organisations and well as talent aspiring to step into the automobile industry. Both the two-wheeler and four-wheeler sector are rapidly growing increasing the demand for job seekers. The automobile industry requires special requirements and skills sets. Our specialised team will head hunt for these niche skill sets suitable for this industry.

E-COMMERCE

Business has changed the way it is conducting its operations. The conventional stores are replaced with online stores. The Internet has paved the way to globalisation and business is spreading its wings across borders. Mobile applications are complimenting the online business. Consumers have switched their perceptions towards purchase and shopping. Many companies have adopted a mix of traditional and e-commerce style of business. E-commerce business has created job opportunities across nations. A company can have multiple presences in different countries. An e-commerce business can be conducted as business to business (B2B), business to consumer(B2C), consumer to business (C2B), intra-business commerce, government to citizen, mobile commerce etc. The e-commerce business has paved the way for many jobs. E-commerce leads to job opportunities in warehousing, logistics, online banking services, procurement, purchases, customer services, back-end technical processes, back-end customer services, etc
